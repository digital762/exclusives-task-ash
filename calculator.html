UAE Mortgage Rules
Expatriates can borrow up to 75% LTV for properties under AED 5M (80% for UAE nationals). Minimum down payment is 25% for expats.
📋
Additional Costs
Budget for 4% DLD fee, 2% agent commission, 0.25% mortgage registration fee, and AED 5,000–10,000 in admin fees.

Current Rates
UAE mortgage rates currently range from 3.99% to 5.5% p.a. Fixed-rate and variable EIBOR-linked products are both widely available.
